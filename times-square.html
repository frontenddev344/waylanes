Times Square Hotels

Experience the Energy of Times Square

Times Square is the loudest, busiest version of Manhattan — bright lights, Broadway theatres, and round-the-clock energy. Staying nearby means everything is walkable, though it’s worth knowing it’s also one of the pricier and more crowded areas to book.

Spend your days exploring the landmarks, catching a show, or shopping along Fifth Avenue, then head back to whichever hotel below fits your trip.
