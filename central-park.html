Explore Central Park

Find a Quiet Moment in the Heart of the City

From peaceful walking paths and open lawns to picturesque bridges and seasonal scenery, Central Park offers a refreshing escape where nature and New York City come together.

Central Park

Experience New York Beyond the Busy Streets

Central Park offers a different perspective on New York City, where winding pathways, shaded gardens, and open green spaces provide a welcome break from the surrounding skyline. Whether you're starting your morning with a walk or taking an afternoon to relax, the park invites you to slow down and enjoy the moment.

Discover scenic lakes, historic bridges, public art, and quiet corners that change with every season. From leisurely strolls to picnics on the Great Lawn, Central Park creates memorable moments for visitors looking to experience a calmer side of the city.

Explore Central Park

Stay Near One of New York City's Most Loved Green Spaces

Central Park is surrounded by some of Manhattan's most distinguished hotels, offering convenient access to walking trails, museums, and neighborhood cafés. Whether you're planning a relaxing escape or a city adventure, these nearby stays place the park just outside your door.

01

The Ritz-Carlton New York, Central Park

Looking directly onto Central Park, this luxury hotel offers an ideal setting for travelers who appreciate peaceful morning views while remaining close to Midtown's shopping, dining, and cultural attractions. The park's southern entrance is only a short walk from the lobby.

02

JW Marriott Essex House New York

Standing along Central Park South, this historic hotel has welcomed visitors for generations. Its location makes it easy to begin the day with a walk through the park before exploring nearby landmarks, museums, and the surrounding Midtown neighborhood.

03

Park Lane New York

Overlooking the southern edge of Central Park, Park Lane New York blends a classic location with contemporary interiors. Guests can step outside for a leisurely stroll through the park or spend the afternoon exploring nearby Fifth Avenue and Columbus Circle.

04

The Plaza Hotel

Situated at the southeastern corner of Central Park, The Plaza Hotel remains one of New York City's best-known landmarks. Its location provides effortless access to the park's walking paths while placing guests within easy reach of shopping, dining, and cultural attractions.

A Peaceful Stay • Near Central Park

Enjoy the City at a Different Pace

Just moments from Central Park, you'll find a quieter side of New York where tree-lined paths, open green spaces, and scenic views offer a welcome change from the city's fast pace. Whether you're visiting for a few days or an extended stay, it's an ideal place to relax between exploring the neighborhoods, museums, and landmarks nearby.
