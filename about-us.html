About Our Story

Where Modern Luxury Meets Timeless Hospitality

We started Waylanes because planning a New York trip is more confusing than it should be. Every neighborhood has dozens of decent hotels, and most travel sites either bury you in ads or push whichever one pays the most. We wanted something simpler: real neighborhoods, honestly compared, so you can pick a base for your trip without the second-guessing.

We’re not a hotel — we’re an independent team that researches New York’s neighborhoods and points you toward hotels worth considering in each one. When you book through a link on our site, we may earn a small commission. It doesn’t cost you anything extra, and it’s the only reason we can keep this free.
