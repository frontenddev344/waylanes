Top Places to Stay in New York

Choosing where to stay can shape your entire trip. Some travelers enjoy the fast-paced energy of Midtown, while others prefer the waterfront views and local charm of Brooklyn. No matter your travel style, New York offers neighborhoods with their own character, attractions, and places to stay. Explore some of the city's most popular areas and find the location that's right for your visit.

Make the Most of Your Time in New York

Finding the right hotel is only the beginning. New York is a city that's meant to be explored, with something new waiting around almost every corner. Whether you're visiting for a few days or staying a little longer, these experiences can help you get a real feel for the city beyond your accommodation.

Nature & Relaxation

Spend a Day in Central Park

In the middle of Manhattan, Central Park offers a welcome break from the city's fast pace. Take a walk beneath tree-lined paths, rent a bike, enjoy a picnic on the Great Lawn, or stop by the Central Park Zoo. It's the perfect place to slow down and enjoy a different side of New York.

Entertainment

See a Broadway Show

A trip to New York isn't complete without experiencing Broadway. From long-running classics to the latest productions, the Theatre District is home to performances that attract audiences from around the world. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a returning theater fan, there's always something worth seeing.

Iconic Landmark

Walk Across the Brooklyn Bridge

Connecting Manhattan and Brooklyn, the Brooklyn Bridge is one of the city's most iconic landmarks. The walk offers incredible views of the skyline, the East River, and the Statue of Liberty in the distance. It's a simple way to experience two of New York's most popular neighborhoods in one outing.

Arts & Culture

Explore New York's Museums

New York is home to some of the finest museums in the world. Spend an afternoon exploring art at The Metropolitan Museum of Art, discover natural history exhibits, or admire modern masterpieces at the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA). No matter your interests, there's a museum that's worth adding to your itinerary.

Shopping

Discover New York's Shopping Districts

Shopping in New York ranges from luxury boutiques to independent local stores. Browse international brands along Fifth Avenue, explore the unique shops of SoHo, or visit Hudson Yards for a mix of fashion, dining, and entertainment. Each neighborhood offers its own shopping experience, making it easy to find something that suits your style.

Why Travelers Love New York

There's a reason millions of people visit New York every year. The city offers something for every kind of traveler, whether you're here to see famous landmarks, enjoy world-class entertainment, explore unique neighborhoods, or simply experience the energy that makes New York unlike anywhere else. No matter how long you're staying, you'll always find something new around the corner.

Famous Landmarks Around Every Corner

From Central Park and the Empire State Building to the Statue of Liberty and Brooklyn Bridge, New York's most iconic sights are never far from where you stay.

Neighborhoods with Their Own Personality

Every New York neighborhood has its own character, from the energy of Manhattan and Times Square to the creative, laid-back vibe of Brooklyn.

Great Food and Entertainment

Whether you're craving street food, fine dining, Broadway shows, or live music, New York offers something to enjoy day and night.

Easy to Explore

With an extensive subway system, buses, and walkable neighborhoods, getting around New York is simple no matter where you're staying.
