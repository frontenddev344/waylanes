Top Places to Stay in New York

Every neighborhood offers a different perspective of New York City. Whether you're looking for iconic attractions, waterfront views, world-class dining, or a quieter place to unwind, explore accommodations in some of the city's most visited areas.

Manhattan

From Central Park and Fifth Avenue to Wall Street and SoHo, Manhattan brings together world-famous landmarks, business districts, museums, restaurants and entertainment.

Explore Manhattan Hotels

Times Square

Stay close to Broadway theatres, bright city lights, shopping, restaurants, and late-night entertainment. Hotels around Times Square provide convenient access to many of New York's most recognizable attractions.

Explore Times Square Hotels

Brooklyn

Known for its waterfront parks, local cafés, art scene, and neighborhoods like Williamsburg and DUMBO, Brooklyn offers a different side of New York while remaining just minutes from Manhattan.

Why Travelers Choose New York

New York continues to attract visitors from around the world with its unique combination of history, culture, entertainment, architecture, and business opportunities. Whether you're here for a weekend getaway or an extended visit, every neighborhood offers something different to explore.

Discover what makes New York one of the world's most exciting destinations.

Iconic Attractions

Visit renowned destinations including Central Park, the Empire State Building, Rockefeller Center, the Brooklyn Bridge, and the Statue of Liberty while staying close to the city's most celebrated landmarks.

Diverse Neighborhoods

Each part of New York has its own character—from the historic streets of Lower Manhattan to the creative energy of Brooklyn and the vibrant atmosphere surrounding Times Square.

Dining & Entertainment

Discover everything from neighborhood cafés and local bakeries to internationally recognized restaurants, Broadway productions, museums, and live music venues throughout the city.

Convenient Transportation

New York's extensive subway system, buses, commuter rail services, and walkable neighborhoods make it easy to reach attractions, airports, shopping districts, and business centers.
